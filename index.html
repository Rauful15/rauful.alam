About Me

I am a dedicated medicinal chemist with a Ph.D. in Organic Chemistry, deeply passionate about driving pre-clinical drug discovery research. My scientific journey is paralleled by a commitment to social activism, where I actively work towards reforming and enhancing the education and research landscape in Bangladesh.

Innovating at the frontier of drug discovery to improve human health.

Championing educational reform and fostering research culture.

Bridging the gap between scientific advancement and societal impact.
