The Future of RNA Splicing in Drug Discovery

RNA splicing modulation represents a paradigm shift in drug discovery, offering unprecedented opportunities to target previously "undruggable" proteins. By manipulating the splicing machinery, we can correct disease-causing mutations at the RNA level, potentially treating genetic disorders like Duchenne muscular dystrophy and spinal muscular atrophy. This approach bypasses traditional protein targeting limitations and opens new therapeutic avenues for rare diseases affecting millions worldwide.
